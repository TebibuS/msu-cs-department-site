Eligibility for Upper Division

To declare the upper-division CIS major, students must:
— Complete all lower-division CIS Core, General Education, Science, and Math requirements listed above.
— Maintain a minimum GPA (per department policy).
— Fulfill any additional departmental prerequisites.
— To get into the upper-division program, the above-given criteria should be fulfilled. For more information, click here.
